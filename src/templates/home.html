About

Welcome to rathethepauper

We strive to fix things faster and make living even more comfortable!

The main aim of this website is to have an overview of how well certain aspects of the house are functioning and maintained as judged by its residents. By rating these aspects (e.g. Internet speed, toilet cleanliness) we gather data and support for argumentation if something is faulty. We then have more collective support to urge our labdlord to fix things quicker.
