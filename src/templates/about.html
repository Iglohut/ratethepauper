What is this website?

My aim is or us all to live comfortably

This website is intended to be used by residents of van Oldenbarneveldstraat 6 and its sub-houses on that street. You can rate aspects (e.g. internet, showers, noise) of our house that are supposed to be well maintained by our landlord. These ratings are depicted with figures to show statistics of how a certain aspect is doing. These statistics can then be usd by our landlord for early detection of faulty apsects. Are there many low ratings for internet this week? Must be a sign! Furthermore, we can use the power of numbers to argue that something is wrong to our landlord.
